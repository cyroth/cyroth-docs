## DOOM
DOOM is an iconic first-person shooter game that redefined the genre with its fast-paced action and immersive gameplay. Players take on the role of a space marine battling hordes of demons from Hell on Mars and its moons.
### Key Features
- **Fast-Paced Action**: Experience intense combat with a variety of weapons and power-ups.
- **Iconic Enemies**: Face off against a diverse array of demonic foes, each with unique behaviors and attack patterns.
- **Atmospheric Levels**: Explore dark and atmospheric environments filled with secrets and challenges.
- **Multiplayer Modes**: Engage in competitive and cooperative multiplayer battles with friends.
### Getting Started
To start your journey in DOOM, visit the [official DOOM website](https://bethesda.net/en/game/doom) to purchase and download the game. Choose your platform, create an account, and dive into the action-packed world of DOOM!
